About Spellcheck.ai

Welcome to Spellcheck.ai!

This tool allows you to paste a paragraph or provide a website URL to check for spelling mistakes.

It uses two powerful methods to identify spelling errors:

  • Dictionary-Based Spellcheck: Checks each word against a standard dictionary.
  • LLM-Based Spellcheck: Uses a Large Language Model (LLM) to detect spelling mistakes and suggest corrections based on context.

To begin, select a spellcheck mode — Dictionary-Based, LLM-Based, or Combined Mode — then paste a paragraph or website URL into the input box on the home page to view spelling corrections based on your choice.

You can also view your previous spellchecks by clicking the clock icon in the sidebar.
